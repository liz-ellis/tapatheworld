About Us

About Restaurant

At Tapa The World, we blend the rich traditions of Spanish tapas with the vibrant spirit of Colorado. Here, you’ll discover a menu that features inventive small plates crafted from the freshest Colorado ingredients, set against a backdrop of warm hospitality and stunning mountain-inspired decor. Whether you’re unwinding after a day of adventure or enjoying a special night out, our commitment is to deliver a uniquely satisfying dining experience that captures the essence of both tapas and our beloved home state.

Tapa The World was founded by Liz and David Ellis, where our restaurant began as a dream shared over countless family dinners and working along side each other in restaurants for over a decade. In 2025, we finally opened our doors with the goal of creating a warm, inviting space where guests could enjoy delicious food and genuine hospitality. Our menu features a blend of Spanish traditions and Colorado cuisine, crafted with the freshest ingredients and a touch of our personal flair. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Our History

The Food

Our menu is a delightful journey through bold flavors and regional specialties. From our succulent lamb chops, perfectly seasoned and grilled to your liking, to our true Colorado-style green chili, that offers a robust and spicy blend, you're sure to find something you'll crave. We keep it local by only serving ingredients found in our home state of Colorado, creating some of the freshest menu items around. Each dish is crafted with care, bringing together the best of traditional and innovative cooking to create a memorable dining experience.
